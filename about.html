About Our Mission

We are dedicated to promoting active citizenship, social transformation, and inclusion through the Erasmus+ programme.

Who We Are

Erasmus+ Youth is a dissemination platform managed by the "Hellas for Us" network. Founded with a mission to promote equality and social inclusion, we focus on the transition to a "Society for All".


We aim to foster inclusive societies that are stable, safe, just, and tolerant. By leveraging the opportunities provided by the Erasmus+ programme, we connect young people from diverse backgrounds to learn, travel, and grow together.

Our Core Values

Inclusion

Ensuring equal opportunities for all, regardless of background or ability.

Innovation

Adopting non-formal education methods to foster creativity and critical thinking.

Solidarity

Building a supportive network that encourages volunteering and active participation.

Erasmus+ Youth

Connecting youth, sharing results, and building a better future.
