About Hellas for Us

Empowering the next generation through innovation and inclusion.

Founded in 2015, Hellas for Us is dedicated to fostering the active and responsible participation of youth in the social, cultural, and educational life of our community. Our mission is to empower young people by encouraging a healthier lifestyle, promoting democratic engagement, and enhancing their creative potential. We are committed to supporting youth and adults, particularly those from rural areas, by providing opportunities for meaningful involvement and personal growth. Hellas for Us strives to create a society where young people are not only participants but also leaders in shaping their communities. We believe in the power of youth to drive positive change, and we are passionate about nurturing their potential through diverse activities that span sports, culture, technology, and civic engagement.

We organize a wide range of initiatives including sports and healthy lifestyles programs through seminars, conferences, and youth exchanges both domestically and internationally, promoting inclusivity for people with fewer opportunities and disabilities. We support cultural and creative expression through bottom-up initiatives that enrich the cultural fabric of our communities while fostering a sense of belonging to the European community. Our work emphasizes democratic engagement and active citizenship, equipping youth with knowledge and skills for democratic participation while advocating for human rights and equality regardless of religion, race, or social background. We promote technological innovation and lifelong learning through non-formal education, experiential learning, and nature-based approaches.

Our methods are developed by professional trainers and educators with extensive experience in youth and adult education. We share knowledge and create a supportive environment using coaching techniques like NLP and lifestyle coaching. The members of our organization are experienced in working with youth and adults, especially people with fewer opportunities, united around the idea of promoting education, European awareness, and active citizenship.
